Scene 1

Shot # Short Description Framing Angle INT/EXT Camera Movement
1 All the other chickens come out, and begin to sing Long Shot Low angle/Camera on the ground EXT Dolly in
2 Ginger gets caught by Mr. Tweedy Medium close up Low Angle and Dutch Angle EXT Tilt up and Dolly in
3 MRS. TWEEDY discovers that EDWINA has not produced any eggs, so takes her off to be killed Close up Shot of EDWINA High angle EXT Dolly in
4 GINGER watches helplessly from the rooftop. Medium Long Shot Eye level EXT Fixed
5 A FIELD OF GRASS Medium close up Eye level EXT Dolly in
6 A FIELD OF GRASS Medium Shot Low angle EXT Rotating around Chicken

Scene 2

Shot # Short Description Framing Angle INT/EXT Camera Movement
1 TWEEDY sees an ad for a pie machine. Very Long Shot Low Angle EXT Fixed
2 GINGER is now presenting the latest escape plan Medium Shot Low Angle INT Dolly in and tilt up
3 All the chickens immediately start cleaning up the room, rushing back to their beds. Long shot Eye Level INT Fixed
4 MR. TWEEDY opens the door to the hut and sees all the hens asleep in their bed. Medium close up Eye Level INT Pan left

Scene 3

Shot # Short Description Framing Angle INT/EXT Camera Movement
1 Frustrated, she punches the fence. Extreme Closeup Eye level EXT Pan left quickly
2 Ginger then hears screaming in the distance and looks up to see Rocky the rooster “flying” above the chicken farm Long Shot Low angle/Look up Angle EXT Camera Swing as breath rhythm
3 Rocky starts to come to his senses. Waking up, he is confused and dazed about his situation. Medium Shot High Angle/Dutch Angle INT Camera zoom out and rotating
4 Rocky continues to confidently speak while walking in the direction of the door Medium Close Up Shot Eye level INT Camera Pan follow Rocky on left side
5 Rocky stops in his tracks and turns to Ginger. Medium Close Up Shot Eye Level INT Camera Follow behind and turn to ginger

Scene 4

Shot # Short Description Framing Angle INT/EXT Camera Movement
1 The chickens start exercising together as a group. Long Shot Eye level EXT Camera track right
2 Suddenly, the ground starts shaking Extreme Close up Shot Low Angle on the ground EXT Camera dolly in
3 The truck closes and starts to drive off, as the chickens look on with binoculars at the machine. Medium Shot Low Angle EXT Camera fixed

Scene 5

Shot # Short Description Framing Angle INT/EXT Camera Movement
1 ROCKY in the center of the coop, surrounded by many chickens. Long Shot Low Angle INT Camera Dolly out
2 GINGER pulls ROCKY by his injured arm off to the side Medium Close Up Shot Eye level EXT Camera pan left
3 They're fattening us up. They're going to kill us all. Close Up Eye level EXT Zoom in
4 GINGER walks out in anger, ROCKY follows. Medium Long Shot Low Angle EXT Fixed

Scene 6

Shot # Short Description Framing Angle INT/EXT Camera Movement
1 GINGER is all by herself on top of one of the coops. Very Long Shot High Angle EXT Camera Dolly out
2 FLETCHER knocks on the radio Big Close Up Eye Level INT Fixed
3 BUNTY’S foot taps to the beat Medium Close Up Eye Level INT Tilt Down
4 ROCKY spins BABS to the other side of the floor. Medium Shot High Angle INT Camera Rotating left around Rocky as spin
5 [Ginger] she accidentally bumps into ROCKY and he begins dancing with her. Medium Shot Eye Level EXT Camera follows Ginger’s step
6 ROCKY takes GINGER and begins to waltz as the rest of the chickens start pairing up. Long Shot Low Angle EXT Fixed

Scene 8

Shot # Short Description Framing Angle INT/EXT Camera Movement
1 The chickens continue to panic, some scream, a few run around, and some hold on, desperately, to their heads. Long Shot High Angle EXT Camera Dolly out
2 ROCKY walks downstage center Medium Close Up Low Angle EXT Camera following Rocky

Scene 9

Shot # Short Description Framing Angle INT/EXT Camera Movement
1 The hens go in on eachother; speculating why Rocky left; accusing one another of bad behavior. Long Shot Eye Level INT Camera moving through crowd
2 Mrs. Tweedy! The chickens are revolting! Close up Low Angle EXT Camera dolly in
3 Full throttle! Medium Close Up Low Angle EXT Camera zoom out
4 Mrs. Tweedy holds her axe in her mouth as she continues to climb up Medium Close Up High Angle EXT Camera swing
5 Mac kisses Fowler, then hugging Babs, then they hug while sobbing, then holding Rocky, then they kiss Medium Shot Eye Level EXT Dolly out and move up

Scene 10

Shot # Short Description Framing Angle INT/EXT Camera Movement
1 The lights are bright. Very long Shot High Angle EXT Camera track right
2 GINGER leans in to hug ROCKY. Medium Close Up Shot Eye level/Camera at right EXT Camera pan right
3 The music fades and the actors take one last bow. Long Shot Low Angle EXT Camera fixed
