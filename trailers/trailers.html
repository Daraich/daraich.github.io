Trailer Proposal

By Renfei Zhang and Shirley You

The trailer represents the most famous scene in the movie Chicken Run and reveals the main idea and the tone of the musical: to escape. The trailer starts with Ginger's scene trying to escape from the farm, which establishes the background story. Mission Impossible Theme serves as background music in the trailer and establishes the tense atmosphere of the movie. Other main characters were introduced in the last half of the trailer with dialogues that represent their personalities. The editing style will be fast-paced cross-cutting in order to show multiple characters and complicated plots within a short video.
