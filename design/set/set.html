Set Design

Created by Lian Bragg, Edited by Raymond Sanchez

Chicken Coop Exterior

  • Rusted chain link fences with barbed wire on top
  • At each corner sits a watch tower
  • The ground inside the fence is concrete

Chicken Coop Interior

  • Large metal/wood barn
    • Looks somewhat like a red barn but is composed of steel/wood
    • Smoke stacks (like those found atop a factory or a steamship) sit atop the barn, constantly emitting fossil fuels

Photos found/chosen by Producer Raymond Sanchez

Tweedy's House Exterior

  • Bleak victorian type house
  • Broken tinted windows with bars on the outside
  • Chipped white paint exterior
  • Bleak shabby/drab minimal landscape/vines growing on exterior of house

Tweedy house photo found/chosen by Producer Raymond Sanchez

Tweedy's House Interior

  • Spider webs in the room corners/window seals
  • Living room empty (as if house is abandoned)
  • Desk area cluttered
  • Kitchen with piled up dirty dishes
  • Moldy food on kitchen table (with bones of a chicken)
  • Dark interior (lighting); dim lights
  • Medieval looking spiral staircase

Pie Machine Room Exterior

  • Dystopian type factory
  • Dome shaped with fumes emitting from top
  • Howard Hugh plane inspired metal exterior

Howard Hughes plane inspiration picture found/chosen by Producer Raymond Sanchez

Pie Machine Room Interior

  • Conveyor belts throughout room constantly in motion
  • Huge metal funnel that drops chicken into a tube of whirling razorblades
  • Separate conveyor belt leads to dough making compartment
    • Consists of three rotating metal bowls that mix the ingredients
  • Inspiration for the cooking room consists of one like a pizza oven
    • Large metal room with flat surfaces that close and heat to 500 fahrenheit

Photo on bottom right found/chosen by Producer Raymond Sanchez

Utopia

  • No buildings or urbanization
  • A paradise island with a lake surrounding
  • Fruit trees of all kinds (adding color)
  • Tall trees for protection
  • Green hills
